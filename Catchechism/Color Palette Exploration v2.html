My Top Picks for Red & Yellow:

🔥 Option 3 (Sacred Flame) — Deep red + amber gold is the most sophisticated and biblical. It feels reverent without being stuffy, and the amber works beautifully for secondary elements.

☀️ Option 4 (Modern Warm) — Coral red + mustard is contemporary and approachable. Great for engaging younger learners while maintaining maturity.

⚡ Option 5 (Fierce Passion) — Only if you want maximum impact. Pure red + bright yellow is striking but aggressive — good for CTAs and achievements, but use sparingly for UI chrome.

**Avoid Option 2 (Vibrant Energy)** — The scarlet + sunny yellow is a bit too Duolingo-ish and might undermine the gravitas of Scripture study.
